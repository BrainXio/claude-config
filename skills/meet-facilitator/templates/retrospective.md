# Template: Retrospective

**Use when:** Sprint retrospective, post-incident review, project wrap-up

**Participants:** All roles (Architect, Critic, Stakeholder) + Driver (facilitator)

**Duration:** 45 minutes

______________________________________________________________________

## Pre-Meeting (Driver)

1. Gather data: What happened this sprint/incident/project
1. Load metrics: Velocity, incidents, user feedback
1. Set psychological safety rules: Blameless, constructive

______________________________________________________________________

## Round 1: Position Loading (10 min)

### Architect

Technical perspective:

- What went well technically
- What technical debt accumulated
- What architecture served us well

### Critic

Risk perspective:

- What almost went wrong
- What incidents occurred
- What warnings were missed

### Stakeholder

User/business perspective:

- What delivered value
- What missed the mark
- What users are asking for

______________________________________________________________________

## Round 2: Generate Insights (20 min)

### All Roles

Use one of these frameworks:

**4Ls:**

- Liked — what we enjoyed
- Learned — what we discovered
- Lacked — what was missing
- Longed for — what we wish we had

**Sailboat:**

- Wind — what pushed us forward
- Anchors — what held us back
- Rocks — what risks we see
- Island — what we're aiming for

**Start/Stop/Continue:**

- Start — new practices to try
- Stop — practices that aren't serving us
- Continue — practices that work well

______________________________________________________________________

## Round 3: Decide Actions (15 min)

### All Roles

Select 1-3 actionable experiments:

- Must be specific (not "communicate better")
- Must have an owner
- Must have a check-in date

Format:

```
Experiment: [what we're trying]
Owner: [who runs it]
Duration: [how long we'll try]
Success metric: [how we'll know it worked]
Check-in: [when we review]
```

### Driver

Document and schedule:

- Add experiments to backlog
- Schedule check-in meeting
- Share retro summary with team
